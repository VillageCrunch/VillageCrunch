# Masala Section - Image Setup Required

## Missing Product Images
You need to add images for the new masala products in the `/backend/frontend/public/Product_Images/` directory:

1. **coriander-powder.jpg** - Coriander powder image
2. **turmeric-powder.jpg** - Turmeric powder image  
3. **jeera-powder.jpg** - Jeera/Cumin powder image
4. **red-chilli-powder.jpg** - Red chilli powder image

## Image Requirements:
- **Format:** JPG/PNG
- **Recommended Size:** 800x800px or similar square ratio
- **Quality:** High-resolution product photos
- **Background:** Clean, preferably white/neutral
- **Content:** Show the powder in attractive packaging or bowls

## Quick Action:
1. Source or take photos of these spice powders
2. Save them with the exact filenames above
3. Upload to `/backend/frontend/public/Product_Images/` directory
4. Products will automatically display the images

## Alternative:
Use placeholder images initially and replace with professional photos later.
